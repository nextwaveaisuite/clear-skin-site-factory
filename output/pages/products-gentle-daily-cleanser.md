# Clear Skin Essentials — Gentle Daily Cleanser
**Soap-Free | pH Balanced | Barrier-Friendly**

## Clean skin without the tight, stripped feeling
Gentle Daily Cleanser is designed for everyday cleansing that supports comfort — especially for people who react to harsh foaming cleansers.

The goal is simple: **clean, comfortable skin** that still feels like skin.

- Sensitive or reactive-prone skin
- Dry, dehydrated, or combination skin
- People who feel “tight” after washing
- Men who shave (comfort-focused cleansing)

## What to expect

- Easy to use morning and night
