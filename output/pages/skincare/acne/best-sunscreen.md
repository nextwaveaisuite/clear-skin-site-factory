# Best Sunscreen for Acne-prone Skin (Australia)

Sunscreen is part of clear skin in Australia. UV exposure can worsen inflammation and post-blemish marks.

## What to look for

- comfortable daily wear
- a product you’ll actually use consistently

## Tips

- moisturiser first if you’re dry or irritated
- patch test and introduce slowly
