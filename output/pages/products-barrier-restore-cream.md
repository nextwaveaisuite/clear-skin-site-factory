# Clear Skin Essentials — Barrier Restore Cream
**Fragrance-Free | Face & Body | Barrier-First**

## Calm. Comfort. Everyday barrier support.
Barrier Restore Cream is a fragrance-free daily moisturiser designed to support the skin barrier and improve overall skin comfort. It’s made for people who want healthy-looking skin without irritation, harsh actives, or unnecessary extras.

Designed for Australian conditions and everyday use, this cream aims for a clean, comfortable finish — **not greasy, not sticky**.

## What it helps with (cosmetic-safe)
- Helps support the skin’s natural barrier
- Helps reduce visible dryness and redness
- Helps improve skin comfort and softness
- Leaves skin looking healthy and balanced

## Who it’s for
- Sensitive or reactive-prone skin
- Dry or dehydrated skin
- Skin that becomes irritated easily
- People who prefer fragrance-free skincare
- Men and women wanting a simple daily moisturiser

## Texture & finish
- Creamy, lightweight texture
- Absorbs easily
- Non-greasy, non-sticky
- Soft, clean, natural finish

## Free from
- Fragrance
- Essential oils
- Harsh exfoliating acids
- Drying alcohols
- Unnecessary colourants

## How to use
Apply a small amount to clean, dry skin morning and night.
Use on face, neck, or dry areas of the body as needed.

**Patch test before first use.**
