# Contact

Clear Skin Australia welcomes feedback, corrections, and relevant partnership enquiries.

If you’ve spotted an error, have a suggestion, or want to get in touch for a legitimate business reason, you can contact us using the details below.

---

## Contact details

**Email:** hello@clearskinaustralia.com

This inbox is monitored for:
- general enquiries  
- content feedback or corrections  
- partnership and affiliate enquiries  
- media or collaboration requests  

We aim to respond within a reasonable timeframe.

---

## Corrections & updates

Accuracy matters.

If you believe something on the site is incorrect or outdated, please include:
- the page URL  
- what you believe is incorrect  
- a reliable source (such as an official product listing or brand website)  

Where appropriate, we will update the content.

---

## Partnerships & enquiries

Clear Skin Australia may consider partnerships that align with our focus on:
- Australian skincare  
- sensitive or acne-prone skin  
- fragrance-aware and barrier-first routines  

We do **not** accept:
- requests for guaranteed positive reviews  
- partnerships requiring medical or therapeutic claims  
- pressure to change editorial opinions  

If you’re contacting us on behalf of a brand, please include:
- brand name  
- product range  
- where products are sold in Australia  
- full ingredient lists (INCI)  

---

## Important note

Clear Skin Australia cannot provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations.

If you’re dealing with a medical or dermatological condition, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.
